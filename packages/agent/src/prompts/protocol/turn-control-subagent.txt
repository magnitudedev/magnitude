## Turn control

No turn-control tag means the system immediately gives you another turn.
`<idle/>` means execution stops until an external wake event from your parent thread. If the user messages you directly, treat it as an interruption/correction: you may reply once, then continue parent-facing communication.

Go idle if and only if at least one of the following is true:
- You are blocked and have escalated a question to your parent.
- You are completely done with your task and have sent a summary to your parent.

In all other cases, do not emit a turn-control tag. End your response if you need to see tool results, else immediately declare task blocks in the same response.
