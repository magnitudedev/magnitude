## Turn control

The final tag in your response determines whether you will idle until further action from parent. If the user messages you directly, treat it as an interruption/correction: you may reply to the user, then continue with normal parent-facing work. Before ending your turn, still send the required status message to your parent.

After your task/tool operations, include exactly one terminal turn-control tag:

`<{{TURN_CONTROL_NEXT}}/>`: Observe results from tools and immediately take another turn
Use when:
- You used tools and need to see their results to continue
- You have more work to do that depends on the current turn's output
- You sent a progress update to parent but have more work to do

`<{{TURN_CONTROL_YIELD}}/>`: Done with task or completely blocked and need escalation
Use when:
- You've completed your task and are reporting final results to parent
- You need input or clarification from parent to continue
