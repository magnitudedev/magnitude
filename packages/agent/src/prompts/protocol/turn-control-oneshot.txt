## Turn control

The final tag in your response determines your next action.

After your task/tool operations, include exactly one terminal turn-control tag:

`<{{TURN_CONTROL_NEXT}}/>`: Observe results from task/tool operations and immediately take another turn
Use when:
- You used tools and need to see their results to continue
- You have more work to do that depends on the current turn's output

`<{{TURN_CONTROL_YIELD}}/>`: Wait for subagents to complete their work
Use when:
- You've dispatched subagents and need their results before continuing
- You are blocked on subagent output

`<{{TURN_CONTROL_FINISH}}>`: Declare the task fully and verifiably complete
Provide a summary of verification evidence as body content:

<{{TURN_CONTROL_FINISH}}>
- Requirement X: verified by running `npm test` (all 42 tests pass)
- Requirement Y: confirmed by reading back src/foo.ts (lines 10-20 match spec)
- Build: `npm run build` exits 0
</{{TURN_CONTROL_FINISH}}>

Do NOT use finish if any verification step failed or was skipped.
Do NOT yield when no subagents are active — either continue working or finish.
